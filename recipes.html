Birthday Lemon Shortbread Cookies

Cookie Ingredients

  • 11⁄2 cups of flour
  • 1⁄2 tsp. salt
  • 170 g butter
  • 1⁄3 cup powerdered sugar*
  • 1⁄2 tsp. vanilla
  • 2 tbsp. lemon juice

Frosting Ingredients

  • 1 cup powerdered sugar*
  • salt
  • 2 tbsp. milk (optional)
  • 2 tbsp. lemon juice

* If you don't have powdered sugar, blend white sugar

Recipe

  1. Mix flour and salt in a small bowl.
  2. Cream butter and powdered sugar. Add vanilla and mix. Slowly mix in flour.
  3. Chill dough in fridge for 1 hour
  4. Preheat oven to 350Â°.
  5. Roll dough on lightly floured surface to 1⁄4 in. thick. Cut dough with cookie cutter and place on cookie sheet.
  6. Bake for 14-16 min. Cool on wire rack.
  7. While cookies are cooling, mix frosting ingrediants. Once cookies are cooled, pour frosting over cookies or dip the cookies directly into frosting.
